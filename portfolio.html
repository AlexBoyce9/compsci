Circle VS Square!

We built a game based on Final Fantasy 7 battle system and it ended up super good! We coded 9 unique moves for the player character and 4 for the boss itself with damage rolls and accuracy stats for each attack. Both players have HP and you have the ability to lose the fight.

We recreated Donkey Kong as more of a platformer for our 1.2.5 project and it was a lot of fun, we added user input so you can actually play the game with a starting screen and a restart button in case you mess up, it was a lot of fun to play and it took a while but enjoy!
